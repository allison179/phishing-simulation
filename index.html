❌ Phishing Email (Fake)

🚩 Red Flags in That Email

  • ⚠️
    Fake Sender DomainThe email came from corphr@gmail.com — a free public Gmail account. Rossari HR exclusively uses @rossari.com addresses.
  • ⚠️
    Urgency & Pressure TacticsThe message created artificial urgency ("complete by close of business today"). This is a classic manipulation technique to stop you from thinking critically.
  • ⚠️
    Request to Click a Suspicious LinkLegitimate Rossari HR systems will never ask you to verify your identity by clicking an unsolicited email link.

🛡️ Your Role in Staying Safe

  • 👁️
    Stay VigilantRemain alert at all times and look for signs of suspicious activity in emails and digital communications.
  • 🔍
    Verify Before You ClickHover over links to check the real destination URL and confirm the sender's full email address matches an official @rossari.com domain.
  • 📣
    Report Suspicious ActivityIf you notice anything unusual, report it to IT immediately at it-helpdesk@rossari.com — do not wait.
  • 🔑
    Enable MFAMulti-Factor Authentication means an attacker who steals your password still cannot log in without your second factor. Enable it on all business systems.
